Who Snows!Group Project

Advanced Software Engineering Project

Advanced Software Engineering Project

Team Members: Benjamin Bittles, Joesph Garrett, Elizabeth Gavino, Emily Ng, Taylor Vasquez

Client Members: Kaleb Chanza, Robbie Hawkins, John Clune, Avery Reichler, Levi Grebenc, Logan Weberg

Who Snows Description: One of the problems that students often face is the havoc that a snow day could cause in planning for assignments, group meetings, or hazardous travel conditions. The clients application, “Who Snows?”, would work to assist students in allowing them to make proper preparations for potential snow day. Using live weather conditions and forecasts alongside previous snow day trends, this app would give the likelihood of an upcoming snow day as well as other trends such as road delays or accidents that have or will likely occur. With this tool being accessible, students will be able to approach the winter season in a safe and informed manner.
